DX90 vs. Fire Extent

This graph shows the relationship between the number of days per California County that the temperature exceeded 90 degrees Fahrenheit and the total acres burned by wildfire. The data represents the period from 2013 to 2020. The r-value is 0.58, which means there is a moderate correlation between DX90 and fire extent.


Sources: California Department of Forestry and Fire Protection (CAL FIRE) National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) Federal Communications Commission (FCC) Census API
